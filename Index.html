What There Is To Know

Aubrey Drake Graham, Born in Canada. He took the world by storm in 2010 with huge number 1 hits with stars such as Rihanna and Lil Wayne.

Drakes career first started off in acting, with small part in Degrassi, Before starting his music career with reord label Young Money along with familiar artists such as Nicki Minaj.

After becoming more of a known artist, Drake released "Take Care", which featured artists such as Rihanna who as also becoming well known.

Following the release of this album, Drakes following album "Nothing was the Same", showed that the rapper was steering his music into a rap genre, as many songs on his previous album reached the charts, whereas only 1 did in the following album."

After a 2 year break, drake returned in 2015 with 2 albums, which got back his strong fanbase and regaining success.

"Views" was one of the most highly anticipated albums of 2016, as he had been talking about it since 2014, and it didnt disappoint, the single "One Dance" was the first song to reach 1 Billion streams on apple music, breaking records being number 1 for record time.

By this point Drake was one of the hottest artist of the century, and his next two albums "More Life" and "Scorpion" showed that Drake was consistently reaching top of the charts with every release, taking the world by storm and becoming one of the biggest names in the music industry.
